Shahrukh khan

Shahrukh Khan born 2 November 1965), often credited as Shah Rukh Khan and informally referred to as SRK , is an Indian film actor. He is often called in the media by the names "badshah","King Khan", "Romance King" and King of Bollywood . Khan has acted in 72 Hindi films in a range of genres from romantic dramas to action thrillers.He has won fourteen Filmfare Awards out of thirty nominations for his contributions to the film industry.

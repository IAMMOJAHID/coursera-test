The Events

Freshers

Freshers welcome by the department is something everyone looks forward to in their first year. It is the first time you get to see, meet, talk, dance with your batchmates with whom you will be studying for the next four years and memories and friendships that will last longer than that. This year will be different for sure but be ready for the fun to be had, memories to be made at ChES' Freshers Welcome 2020

Foreign intern ke fundae

“Foreign Intern ke Fundae” session was successfully organized by ChES with the aim of briefing students about the whole process of “Research Internships”.Owing to deep curiosity, buzzing questions and extreme interest in research internships ,students not only from the Chemical Engineering Department but others too, showed up in large numbers. The panelists were none-other than our very own seniors who had bagged the Research Internship in a Foreign University.

Foreign intern ke fundae

Farewell

Every year we bid goodbye to our students who work so hard to get through these four or five years. For a student, the farewell becomes an event of substantial importance. It becomes the official alarm for him/her that s/he is going to leave their second home in a few days. The journey that they go through is no less than a roller coaster ride. For the first time, they experience a life which is filled with failures, and rejections which they might have not faced in their school life.
